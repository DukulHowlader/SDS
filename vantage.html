Introduction:

       Our app name is vantage. This app is designed for teacher and students. Teacher can monitor the reading time that one student spend in the lecture through our app. Teachers create        classes in our app and upload their lectures. Student can read lectures through our app. Our app will work through students eye scan. App will detect students eye and count the time        they spend reading the lectures.
